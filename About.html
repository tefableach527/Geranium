Who we are

At Geranium, we believe in the power of nature to nourish and transform your skin. Our products are handmade in small batches using pure botanical ingredients, free from harsh chemicals and cruelty. We are committed to creating clean, effective skincare you can trust. 

Geranium was born from a simple idea: skincare and hair care should be honest, gentle, and inspired by nature. We carefully craft each formula with love and respect for your skin and the environment. From sourcing sustainable ingredients to using eco-friendly packaging, every detail reflects our promise to help you glow naturally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make skin & Hair care rituals simple, pure, and effective. Geranium blends time-tested botanicals with modern formulations to deliver visible results without compromising on integrity. Because your skin deserves care that’s as kind as it is powerful.
